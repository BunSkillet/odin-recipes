Roast Chicken

Description

Ingredients

  • Whole chicken
  • Garlic and herb seasoning
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Rub the chicken with olive oil and season generously with garlic and herbs.
  2. Place the chicken in a tray and cook in a preheated oven at 180c (fan-forced) for about 1.5 hours.
  3. Rest for 10 minutes before carving to keep it juicy.
