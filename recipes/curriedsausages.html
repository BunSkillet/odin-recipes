Curried Sausages

Description

Curried sausages are a hearty, comforting dish made with thick, juicy sausages simmered in a rich, mildly spiced curry sauce. Often served with creamy mashed potatoes or steamed rice, this classic meal blends savoury flavours with a hint of sweetness from onions and tomato, creating a warming favourite perfect for chilly nights or nostalgic dinners.

Ingredients

  • 500g Thick sausages
  • 1tbsp Curry powder
  • 1 Brown onion

Steps

  1. Brown the sausages in a pan, then slice into bit-sized pieces. to the side.
  2. Saute chopped onion until soft, then stir in curry powder and cook until fragrant.
  3. Add sausages back into the pan with a splash of water or stock and simmer until the sauce thickens.
