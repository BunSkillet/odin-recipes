Lasagna

Description

Ingredients

  • Beef mince
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Bechamel sauce
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion and garlic
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Brown the beef mince with onion and garlic, then add tomato sauce and simmer.
  2. Layer meat sauce, lasagna sheets, and bechamel in a baking dish.
  3. Top with cheese and bake until golden and bubbling.
